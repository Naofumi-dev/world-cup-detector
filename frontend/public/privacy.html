Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What this site is

World Cup Detector is a free football statistics site. It shows match-outcome probabilities, Elo ratings, and tournament simulations computed from a public dataset of historical international matches.

Not betting advice

All predictions are the output of a statistical model and are provided for entertainment and educational purposes only. They are not betting, financial, or professional advice, and no outcome is guaranteed.

Data we collect

  • No account is required and we do not ask for personal information.
  • We use privacy-friendly, aggregate web analytics (page views, country, device type) to understand traffic. This does not identify you personally.
  • Standard server logs (IP address, user agent) may be kept briefly by our hosting providers for security and abuse prevention.

Cookies and advertising

The site itself does not set tracking cookies. If advertising is shown, third-party ad providers (such as Google AdSense) may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can learn how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites and opt out of personalised advertising at adssettings.google.com.

Third-party services

The site is hosted on Vercel (frontend) and a VPS (API). Donations, if offered, are processed entirely by the donation platform (e.g. Ko-fi) — we never see your payment details.
